My Noodles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Sauce
  • Vegetables

Recipe:

  1. Boil water in a pan
  2. Add noodles to the boiling water with salt and oil
  3. Drain the water and keep the noodles aside
  4. Add vegetables to the pan and saute them
  5. Add the boiled noodles to the pan
  6. Add sauce to the noodles and mix well
  7. Serve hot
